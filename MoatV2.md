## The 6 Revolutionary Features

### 1. WritingDNA as 5th Signal

Complete the identity pentagon. Ibis WritingDNA → Starforge integration:

- Nommo panel gets a 5th hub card: **Writing DNA**
- Cross-modal coherence now includes writing style alignment
- Someone who writes in short, percussive sentences AND listens to minimal techno AND photographs brutalist architecture = **extreme coherence**
- Someone who writes lush prose but listens to harsh noise = **creative tension** (equally valuable signal)
- Quantitative metrics (sentence length, TTR, dialogue ratio) + qualitative patterns (tone, cadence, syntax, motifs, influences)
- Voice signature paragraph from Claude Sonnet analysis

### 2. Generative Identity Narrative (Nommo's True Purpose)

Nommo is the Dogon concept of the power of the word. The panel should **speak your identity into existence**:

- LLM generates a 2-3 paragraph identity portrait from ALL signals (Audio + Visual + Writing + Project + Archetype + Coherence)
- Not "You are SCHISM (Contrarian)" — instead: *"You build at the intersection of what shouldn't work together. Your sonic palette pulls from the deep sub-bass of grime but your visual taste reaches toward the clean geometry of De Stijl. This isn't contradiction — it's a controlled collision. You don't follow taste; you manufacture friction and extract meaning from the sparks."*
- This is the **viral mechanic**. People share this. Nobody shares "Openness: 78th percentile"
- Re-generate as your profile evolves → the narrative *changes* with you
- Saved and versioned alongside the profile snapshot

### 3. Temporal Drift Detection

The single most powerful differentiator. No psychometric system tracks identity over time:

- **Profile versioning** — every time signals update, save a timestamped snapshot of the full identity state
- **Drift calculation** — compare archetype distributions, coherence scores, sonic palette, visual DNA across snapshots
- **Influence absorption tracking** — "After uploading 40 tracks from [artist], your sonic palette shifted 15% toward [direction]"
- **Creative season detection** — classify phases: expansive (new genre exploration + rising incoherence), consolidating (narrowing + rising coherence), pivoting (sharp direction change)
- This is what makes people come back. Big Five is a one-time test. Ori becomes a **creative mirror that updates**.

### 4. Drift Visualisation UI

Make temporal drift tangible and beautiful:

- **Timeline view** — horizontal axis = time, vertical = archetype distribution stacked area chart
- **Coherence trajectory** — line chart of cross-modal coherence score over time
- **Sonic palette evolution** — 5-band frequency visualization morphing over time
- **Key moments** — annotated points where significant shifts occurred ("Uploaded 30 jazz tracks", "Subtaste retake shifted from SCHISM to VAULT")
- **Season labels** — "Expansion Phase (Jan-Mar 2026)" / "Consolidation Phase (Apr 2026)"
- Exportable as image for sharing

### 5. Relational Intelligence (B2B TAM Expansion)

Activate the archetype relational dynamics that already exist (collaborators, tensions, growth fields):

- **Duo/Team profiling** — "Upload both libraries. Here's where you align, where you'll clash, and what you'll make that neither could alone"
- **Complementarity score** — quantified creative chemistry based on archetype alignment + sonic palette overlap + visual DNA distance
- **Label roster analysis** — "Your 5 artists have these taste overlaps and gaps. You need a VOID archetype to balance"
- **Brand matching** — "This brand's visual DNA aligns 73% with your aesthetic. Authentic collab potential: high"
- **A&R scouting** — "This unsigned artist's trajectory matches [successful artist] from 3 years ago"
- Turns Ori from personal tool → B2B platform. TAM expansion from $0 → $4.5B+ psychometric market.

### 6. Conviction-Weighted Signals (Unfakeable Profiles)

Integrate Crucibla/Palmlion signals to weight identity by actual investment:

- Money spent on music > saves > streams > views
- Time creating > time consuming
- Repeat listens > first listens
- Sharing with context > mindless repost
- Tips (Oryx/Palmlion) are the strongest signal
- The archetype is weighted by what you actually *invest in*, not what you claim to like
- Kills the fundamental weakness of every questionnaire: **you can't game it because it's measuring behavior, not self-report**

What makes it defensible is NOT the psychometric validation (that comes with traction) — it's the **multi-modal behavioral data** that no questionnaire can replicate.

MBTI has zero scientific validity and a $2B market. It went viral because "INFJ" is an identity badge. Ori goes viral because it generates a **paragraph that sounds like you** from data that proves who you actually are.

The question isn't the science. It's the distribution. Build the mirror that updates, and people will keep looking.
